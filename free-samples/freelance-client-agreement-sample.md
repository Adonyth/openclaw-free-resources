### 1. Scope of Work

Freelancer agrees to provide the following services:

> [Describe the project clearly and specifically. Example: "Design and develop a 5-page marketing website using WordPress, including homepage, about, services, blog, and contact pages. Includes up to 2 rounds of revisions."]

---

### 2. Timeline

- **Project start date:** ___________________
- **Expected completion date:** ___________________
- **Key milestones (if applicable):** ___________________

Timelines are contingent on Client providing necessary materials, feedback, and approvals within [3 business days / specify] of each request.

---

### 3. Payment

- **Total project fee:** $___________________
- **Deposit due at signing:** $___________________ ([50]% of total)
- **Final payment due:** Upon project completion / delivery of final files

**Late payments** beyond [14] days will incur a [1.5]% monthly late fee.

Freelancer reserves the right to pause work if payment is more than [7] days overdue.

---

### 4. Intellectual Property & Ownership

Upon receipt of full payment, all work product created under this Agreement becomes the sole property of the Client. Until full payment is received, Freelancer retains all rights to the work.

Freelancer may display the completed work in their portfolio unless Client requests otherwise in writing.

---

### 5. Revisions

This Agreement includes up to [2] rounds of revisions. Additional revision rounds will be billed at $[___] per hour.

---

### 6. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any non-public information shared during the course of this project and not to disclose it to third parties without written consent.

---

### 7. Independent Contractor Status

Freelancer is an independent contractor, not an employee of Client. Freelancer is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and business expenses.

---

### 8. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with [7] days written notice. Upon termination:
- Client owes payment for all work completed through the termination date
- The deposit is non-refundable if Client terminates without Freelancer fault
- Freelancer will deliver all completed work files upon receipt of outstanding payment

---

### 9. Limitation of Liability

Freelancer's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client under this Agreement. Freelancer is not liable for indirect, consequential, or incidental damages.

---

### 10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [State / Province / Country]. Any disputes will be resolved through good-faith negotiation first, then binding arbitration if necessary.
