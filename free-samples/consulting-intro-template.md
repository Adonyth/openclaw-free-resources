# Consulting Engagement Templates

Two templates in one file: (1) an intro email to kick off a new engagement, and (2) a letter of engagement outline to formalize the relationship.

---

## Template 1: Consulting Engagement Intro Email

*Use this email after a verbal agreement or signed proposal — it sets the tone, establishes next steps, and keeps everyone aligned from day one.*

---

**Subject:** Getting Started — [Project Name] Engagement

Hi [Client First Name],

I'm looking forward to working with you on [project/initiative name]. This note is to confirm our engagement details and outline how we'll get started.

**Engagement Summary**

- **Scope:** [One-sentence description of what you're being hired to do]
- **Engagement period:** [Start date] through [End date or "ongoing, reviewed monthly"]
- **Primary contact on your end:** [Client contact name and role]

**Next Steps**

1. Please sign and return the Letter of Engagement attached to this email by [date].
2. I'll send a brief onboarding questionnaire to gather background information — please complete it within [3–5 business days].
3. Our kickoff call is scheduled for [date/time]. I'll send a calendar invite shortly.

**How to Reach Me**

- Email: [your email] — I respond within one business day
- For urgent matters: [phone / alternate contact]

Please don't hesitate to reach out with any questions before our kickoff. I want to make sure we hit the ground running.

Looking forward to it,

[Your Name]
[Your Title / Firm Name]
[Website | LinkedIn]

## Template 2: Letter of Engagement — Outline

*A letter of engagement is a lighter-weight alternative to a full consulting contract. Use it for smaller engagements, ongoing advisory work, or when a formal contract is overkill.*

---

**LETTER OF ENGAGEMENT**

**Date:** ___________________

**Consultant:** [Your Name / Firm], [City, State]

**Client:** [Client Name / Company], [City, State]

---

**1. Purpose of Engagement**

[Consultant Name] is engaged to provide [description of consulting services — e.g., "strategic marketing advisory services focused on go-to-market planning for the Q3 product launch"].

---

**2. Deliverables**

The following will be delivered under this engagement:

- [ ] [Deliverable 1 — e.g., "Written go-to-market strategy document"]
- [ ] [Deliverable 2 — e.g., "Two 60-minute strategy sessions per month"]
- [ ] [Deliverable 3 — e.g., "Final recommendations report at engagement close"]

---

**3. Term & Fees**

- **Engagement period:** [Start date] to [End date]
- **Fee structure:** [ ] Hourly ($___/hr) [ ] Monthly retainer ($___/mo) [ ] Fixed project fee ($**_**)
- **Invoicing schedule:** [Weekly / Bi-weekly / Monthly / At milestones]
- **Payment terms:** Net [15/30] days from invoice date

---

**4. Confidentiality**

Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary information shared during this engagement.

---

**5. Independent Contractor**

Consultant operates as an independent contractor. This letter does not create an employment relationship.

---

**6. Acceptance**

By signing below, both parties agree to the terms outlined in this letter.

|-----------|--------|
| Signature: ___________________ | Signature: ___________________ |
| Date: ___________________ | Date: ___________________ |
